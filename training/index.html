About this training

Effective Distributed Teams in Government provides government teams with strategies, tools, and best practices for working effectively in a distributed (remote/telework) model. The offering is customizable to your agency and will help you improve team performance, streamline communications, save on operational costs, and boost workforce diversity and retention.

Who it's for

  • Government team leaders/members
  • Government contractors

Skills you will gain

  • Communications
  • Meeting etiquette
  • Project management

Format

  • Discovery to get aligned on your agency's needs and expected outcomes
  • Preparation to get your teams ready for virtual training
  • Three interactive, virtual classroom sessions
  • Workbook to support learning and for future reference
  • Options for 1:1 personalized coaching
  • Options for telework policy development / improvement

Curriculum

  • Setting up and getting started with remote work
  • Communications and collaboration
  • Security and operations
  • Team cohesion, trust, and accountability
  • Growing and sustaining distributed teams
  • Improving team performance and resilience

Skill level

Beginner/intermediate

Time to complete

3 hours
